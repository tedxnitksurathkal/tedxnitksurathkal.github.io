Welcome to the TEDx 3D Pac-Man Challenge

Game Introduction

Experience a classic reborn in 3D! This unique version of Pac-Man challenges you to navigate a maze from a first-person perspective. Your goal is to clear the maze of all dots while skillfully avoiding the relentless ghosts. It's a test of navigation and quick decision-making in a fully 3D world.

Game Rules & Controls

  • Controls: Use the W, A, S, D keys to control Pac-Man.
  • W: Move Forward
  • S: Move Backward
  • A: Turn Left
  • D: Turn Right
  • Objective: Eat all the small dots in the maze to complete the level.
  • Power Up: Eat one of the four large, flashing "Power Pellets". This will temporarily turn the ghosts white, allowing you to hunt them for bonus points!
  • Game Over: The game ends if a ghost catches you. You only have one life, so be careful! Your score will reset for the next attempt.

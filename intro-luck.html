Game Introduction

Take flight in a world where transformation is key. Guide your bird—a metaphor for the human spirit—through an ever-shifting landscape. Inspired by alchemy, each gate you pass symbolizes a stage of self-evolution, a step toward mastering the balance between chaos and harmony. Beware: hesitation leads to a fall, while perfect rhythm allows transcendence. How far can you soar in your journey of transformation?

Game Rules

  • Tap or click to keep your bird afloat—each flap fuels your ascent.
  • Navigating through narrow gaps requires precision and control, much like refining raw matter into gold.
  • Each successful passage represents an alchemical stage—your progress is a mark of mastery.
  • Colliding with obstacles signifies imbalance—recalibrate and try again!
  • Fly as far as possible to complete your metamorphosis and embrace creative alchemy!

Pause to Play

A "Pause" is a brief interruption that delivers two choices: To catch some breath and continue along the same path or retrace steps and carve out a new one.

The theme revolves around the idea of pausing to reflect upon one's actions and implementing changes in that timeframe in order to continue playing the game called life. "Pauses" are projected as preparatory phases that enable breaking the chain of not-so-fortunate events in "play". Pauses can be due to obstacles strewn in our paths that are meant to teach us lessons along the way or can be self imposed; it is meant to strengthen our skills, to adapt better and evolve.

TEDxNITKSurathkal 2021 aims to provide a platform for self introspection through influential talks filled with narratives that fuels the opting of right combination of pauses and plays.

"Pause to Play" signifies that the motive of each pause should be to continue the play. The idea is to motivate the audience to continue the hustle with added experience and knowledge derived from the valuable inputs of our speakers.
